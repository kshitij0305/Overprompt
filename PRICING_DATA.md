## ChatGPT

| Plan | Price |
|---|---|
| Free | $0/month |
| Go | ~$5/month |
| Plus | $20/month |
| Pro | $200/month |

**Positioning**
- General-purpose conversational AI platform
- Strong fit for mixed workflows including coding, writing, research, and productivity
- Higher tiers target power users, advanced reasoning, and heavy daily usage

**Key Features**
- GPT-5.5 access
- Advanced reasoning models
- Image generation
- Deep research and agent workflows
- Projects, tasks, and custom GPTs
- Expanded memory and context
- Codex integration and coding assistance

**Audit Considerations**
- Premium tiers may be excessive for lightweight conversational workflows
- Teams paying for multiple premium individual subscriptions may have redundant spend
- Advanced plans are most cost-efficient for high-frequency professional usage
- Organizations using overlapping conversational AI platforms may benefit from consolidation

## Claude

| Plan | Price |
|---|---|
| Free | $0/month |
| Pro | $17/month annually ($20 monthly) |
| Max | From $100/month |

**Positioning**
- Strong focus on long-context reasoning and writing-heavy workflows
- Positioned for research, productivity, and knowledge work
- Higher tiers emphasize increased usage limits and advanced capabilities

**Key Features**
- Chat on web, iOS, Android, and desktop
- Code generation and data visualization
- Research capabilities
- Memory and connectors
- Claude Code and integrations
- Slack and Microsoft 365 integrations
- Extended thinking for complex work

**Audit Considerations**
- High-tier Claude plans may exceed lightweight conversational usage requirements
- Premium plans are most valuable for research-heavy and long-context workflows
- Teams using Claude alongside multiple conversational AI tools may have overlapping functionality
- Advanced plans are better justified for heavy daily usage and collaborative environments
